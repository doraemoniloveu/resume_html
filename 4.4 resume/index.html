Summary

Ms. Irene Yuk Ying Ho is a research assistant at the Institute of Health Equity. She received her Bachelor’s degree in Statistics from the Chinese University of Hong Kong. Ms. Ho assisted in a multi-cohort study that analyzed the impact of social isolation on functional disability in older people, as well as the projection of healthy life expectancy in Hong Kong. She is interested in applying various statistical methods to analyze social research questions.
